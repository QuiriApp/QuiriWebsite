Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay with cash? →

You can pay via cash directly at the car wash.

Can I schedule multiple cars? →

Yes, you can schedule multiple vehicles through the app.

How do I cancel or reschedule a booking? →

You can easily cancel or reschedule through the app's booking management section.

Is my payment information secure? →

Yes, all payment information is encrypted and securely processed by a licensed Payment service provider.

How quickly can i get my refund after cancelling a booking? →

Refunds are processed immediately. You will receive your funds within minutes, any day of the week.

What happens when i miss my appointment? →

Reschedule your booking or cancel for a refund. A small penalty fee will be deducted if you choose the refund.
